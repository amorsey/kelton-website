Solar Street Light

Until now Solar Streetlights have been available as a utilitarian solution and have forced designers to choose between form and function.

Lights

Kelton Energy has integrated the components required to support Grid Hybrid lighting into a singulaly elegant design blending the Solar Panel, Led Light Engine and Battery System into a form worth of display in the most distinctive of communities. The patented linear, minimal wind loading bifacial cell array sets this structure apart from any existing solar lighting solutions avaliable to date. Although it's beauty sets it apart, it's processor based solar controller complements it's elegance.

Technology

Our lights all share a robust, micro-processor based core coupled with a Mesh network communications engine allowing simultaneous and configurable lighting operation and instantaneous fault notification. the processor core harvests all of the available solar light using a MPPT charge algorithm and then stores it in a LifePo4 batter pack housed within the light pole allowing a service live of over 3000+ charge cycles. Out Unique Grid Hybrid controller algorithm, when used to upgrade grid based lighting will provide peace of mind coupled with the efficiency of solar lighting. The controller will run off collected solar energy and use the grid for back up during times of poor solar collection to provide virtaully 100% up time.
